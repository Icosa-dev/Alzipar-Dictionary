# Alzapar Miscelaneous: Semi-Irregular Words (Kilam Samigarib)

Semi-irregular words (Kilám Súbagaríb) are words which are neither fully regular or irregular. They follow the trinconsonantal root system (unlike irregular words) however in ways which are not usual of other regular words. The following is all of the semi-irregular words of the language.

The singular (material and abstract) noun form of B-J-T is *bajt*.

The word for monarch and king is both *Milák* from the root M-L-K. The feminine form is *Miláka*.
