# Alzapar Morphology: Noun Forms

Verbs formed from a triconsonantal root (1-2-3) which general meaning (X) may take the following forms:

|   Form   |     Noun      |     Meaning                            |
|----------|---------------|----------------------------------------|
|    (N0)  |      N/A      |   Irregular non-rooted form            |
|    (N1)  |  1i2a패3/1a2a3  |   Singular noun form                   |
|    (N2)  |  1u2a3        |   Plural noun form                     |
|    (N4)  |  1a2i패3        |   Someone who does (X)                 |
|    (N5)  |  1a2i패3un      |   Plural of agent form                 |
|    (N6)  |  Ma12a3       |   Place where (x) is done              |
|    (N7)  |  Ma1a2i패3      |   Plural of locational form            |

The N4 form is also used as the adjectival form.
