# Kitabun Aṡ-Ṡaharin Al-Ażaparin: Ṡiharun Al-Lejlain Al-Nofsin Min-Safoin

## Alżapar
Satara al-lunaun u al-plihediun, <br>
al-isaun al-lejlain al-nofsin, <br>
jaṡavaz hinun, <br>
ima ma-jusnana mirun iżolat. <br>

## Literal Translation
Set-PRES the-moon-NOM and the-pleiades-NOM, <br>
The-time-NOM the-night-GEN the-middle-GEN, <br>
Flowing-PRES time-NOM, <br>
But with-sleep-PRES me-NOM alone.

## Non-Literal Translation
The moon and the Pleiades have set, <br>
it is midnight, <br>
time is passing, <br>
but I sleep alone.
